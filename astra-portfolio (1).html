The Season

Our Journey

From first concept sketch to competition day — the five phases that brought the Astra Mk.I to life.

Phase 01 — Kickoff
Team Formation & Research
Roles assigned, regulations studied in depth, competitive benchmarking of past portfolios completed. Design brief established with performance targets and brand direction set.
Phase 02 — Design
CAD Modelling & Aerodynamic Analysis
Multiple concept iterations developed in Autodesk Inventor. Virtual wind tunnel analysis evaluated and refined the car's aerodynamic form. Final design locked after three revision rounds.
Phase 03 — Manufacturing
CNC Machining & Finishing
CAM toolpaths generated and validated. CNC machining completed with precision tolerances. Extensive post-processing: sanding 120 to 2000 grit, priming, and multi-coat livery painting.
Phase 04 — Branding
Livery, Pit Display & Portfolios
Team brand finalised. Car livery applied. Pit display designed and printed. Engineering and enterprise portfolios written, designed, and reviewed against judging criteria.
Phase 05 — Competition
Regional Finals & Beyond
Astra competes at the Regional Finals — presenting portfolios, undergoing scrutineering, racing head-to-head, and delivering verbal presentations. Eyes firmly set on the National Finals.
